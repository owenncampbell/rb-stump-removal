Most Popular

Stump Grinding

Our primary service — we use our Carlton SP5014 professional stump grinder to reduce stumps to wood chips below the soil surface. This eliminates tripping hazards, prevents regrowth, and frees up your yard for landscaping, seeding, or construction.

  • Ground 6–12 inches below grade
  • Works on stumps of any size
  • No chemicals — purely mechanical removal
  • Wood chips left on-site for natural mulch
  • Suitable for all tree species
Root Clearing

Surface Root Removal

Exposed surface roots are a hazard to mowers, foot traffic, and landscaping. We grind away visible roots that extend from the stump, leaving the surrounding area smooth and safe. This is ideal when you want to reclaim a lawn or garden bed.

  • Protects mower blades and equipment
  • Eliminates tripping hazards
  • Restores lawn usability
  • Pair with stump grinding for full clearance

How to Measure Your Stump

When you call or fill out our quote form, it helps to know your stump's diameter. Here's how to measure correctly:

1
Find the widest point

Look at the stump from above and identify the widest span across the top.

2
Measure straight across — not around

Use a tape measure to get a straight-line diameter, not the circumference. This is the number we need.

3
Count your stumps

If you have multiple stumps, measure each one and note the count. It helps us give you an accurate quote faster.

4
Call or submit online
